Our Story

Behind the Tool

Simple, fast, and privacy-focused utility for everyone.

MakeAList was born out of a simple need: a way to create and manage lists without the friction of account sign-ups, heavy apps, or intrusive tracking. We believe productivity tools should be as immediate as a piece of paper but as powerful as modern web technology.

Our Mission

To provide a free, zero-bloat platform for organizing daily life. Whether you're planning a grocery run, managing a project, or preparing a mailing list, MakeAList is designed to stay out of your way and let you focus on what matters.
