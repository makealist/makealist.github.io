How To Use

How to Create Your Bucket List

A bucket list is one of the most powerful tools for living a purposeful life. Writing down your dreams makes them real, trackable, and achievable. Here's how to make the most of this bucket list maker.

1

Add a Goal

Type any goal, dream, or experience you want to have. It could be grand (climb Kilimanjaro) or personal (learn to bake sourdough). Add a description to capture why it matters to you.

2

Choose a Category

Categorize your goal as Travel, Adventure, Learning, Career, Wellness, Creative, Social, or Finance. This helps you see patterns in your dreams and ensures balance across life areas.

3

Set a Priority

Mark goals as a Dream (long-term aspiration), Planning (actively working toward), or Doing Soon (happening in the near future). This gives your list meaningful direction.

4

Mark as Achieved

When you complete a goal, click the circle checkbox to mark it as Achieved. The date of completion is recorded and your progress percentage updates instantly.

About

Why You Need a Bucket List

Research consistently shows that people with written goals are significantly more likely to achieve them. A bucket list takes this further — it's not just about goals for this year, but for your entire life. It forces you to think about what truly matters to you, what experiences would make you feel fully alive, and what legacy you want to create.

MakeAList's Bucket List Maker is designed to be beautifully simple. There's no complex planning framework to learn. You write your dreams down, organize them by category and priority, and start checking them off. The visual progress bar and achievement percentage make your journey tangible and motivating.

The categories — Travel, Adventure, Learning, Career, Wellness, Creative, Social, and Finance — are carefully chosen to help you build a balanced and rich life, not just one that optimizes for one area. Many bucket lists focus purely on travel or adventure, but the most fulfilled lives include creative achievements, deep relationships, personal growth, and financial freedom too.

Your bucket list is saved automatically. Come back to it regularly — add new dreams as they occur to you, update priorities as life changes, and celebrate every time you get to mark something as Achieved.
