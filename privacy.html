Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. We don't even have a database to store it in.

1. Data Storage

MakeAList is a client-side application. This means that any list you create, items you add, or settings you change are stored locally in your web browser's Local Storage. We do not transmit your list content to our servers, nor do we store it in a central database.

2. Information We Collect

Because MakeAList does not require an account or sign-up, we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We may use basic, anonymized analytics to understand site traffic, but this does not include the content of your private lists.

3. Sharing Lists

When you use the "Share" feature, the application generates a unique URL containing the list data in an encoded format. If you share this link with others, anyone with the link can view the contents of that specific list. We do not store these shared links.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. We only use browser storage (Local Storage) which is necessary for the technical functionality of saving your lists between sessions.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
